Ziggurat

Brandon Austin - David Berry - Travis Fort - Brian Korbus

Ziggurat was created for the 2014 Otronicon Game Jam, winning first place in the competition. Ziggurat is an atmospheric puzzle platformer that takes full advantage of what the Oculus Rift has to offer. You must ascend an ancient temple and escape with the treasure, and your life. For this project Brandon Austin acted as the lead programmers as well as the software configuration manager. Brandon Austin also managed the Oculus Rift integration with Unity3D.

Oculus Rift Enabled



Mommy

Brandon Austin - Jeremy Boggs - David Berry - Leo Araque - Lloyd Brooks

Mommy is a game that plays with your head, and forces you to see the world from a different perspective. You play as a small child trying to figure out why your house is empty and your family missing. It has optional Oculus Rift support and becomes much more impressive when wearing one. As a young child, everything in the environment is larger than you are, which serves to compound the tension.

Oculus Rift Enabled



ZeroGravity

Brandon Austin - James Alleman

With this game we wanted to provide an immersive story using environment design and innovative controls within the Unity 4 game engine using the Oculus Rift. As a player your goal is to attempt to reach your space station which is currently being attacked by an unknown force. This will require you to pilot a spacecraft, navigate a debris field using only a personal jetpack, and find your goal within the labyrinthian space station.

Oculus Rift Enabled
